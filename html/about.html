Hi, I'm Lydia!

In 2015, I began studying psychology at Martin Luther University of Halle-Wittenberg. In my undergraduate studies, I particularly enjoyed the courses that dealt with basic disciplines of psychology. With this in mind, I decided to pursue a career in research. My current master's program in cognitive science at the University of Potsdam is preparing me for this goal by offering more courses in neuroscience, experimental methods, programming, and data analysis. I am looking forward to entering the world of science!
