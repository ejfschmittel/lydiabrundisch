Welcome to my website!

I am a master's student of cognitive science at the University of Potsdam. I plan to report regularly on my work in cognitive, behavioral, and neuroscience research projects and to review some older projects. In particular, I am interested in memory, perceptual, and emotional processing, interindividual differences, and state of the art methods in psychiatry and neurology. In my spare time, I dabble in coding challenges, game design, behavioral biology, metascience, sociology of subcultures, quantified self, pixelart, pen-and-paper role-playing games, playing guitar and harmonica, and jugger.
